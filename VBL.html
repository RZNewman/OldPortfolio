Fillingbird

Design a product (watering can and planter) around keywords (natural, wild, sturdy)

Overview:

Fillingbird was a project to demonstrate visual branding in everyday objects. The final design was based on a hummingbird and sunflower.

Initial:

I sketched multiple cans and planters with a focus on mimicking plants while still retaining the necessary shapes of a can. The shapes of some led to the idea of a humming bird.

>

Work:

I made various deliverables such as image boards, colorized sketches, and a logo.
