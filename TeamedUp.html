TeamedUp

Develop an application that allows the parents of children on sports teams to view information about the team and to contact each other.

Overview:

TeamedUp allows users to view the information of players and parents on teams they are associated with. Additionally, they can view game time and locations, and post public messages to the teams they are on.

Initial:

>

We were given the idea for the project by our group of clients, and helped them break down the technical components and estimate what could be done in the five weeks.

Work:

>

Using weekly development cycles, we iterated on our clientâ€™s ideas and implemented their designs. We built the application in Ionic.

Product:

Our application displayed information on all the team members, allowed communication via a message board in the app, and routed to other apps for call, text, and mapping functions.

Future:

Our application had no way for users to put information into the app, aside from the messaging function. The next step would be to allow user generated, one-time links to be distributed to other users to join existing teams, as well as a web interface to allow entering data on a computer.
